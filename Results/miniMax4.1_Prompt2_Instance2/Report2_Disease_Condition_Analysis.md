## Overview

This report catalogs all disease and medical condition mentions identified in the OCR text of 27 undergraduate research posters. Mentions were identified through keyword matching (case-insensitive) against a curated list of disease and condition terms. Each entry records the disease/condition name, the number of posters mentioning it, and the specific posters in which it was found.

Conditions are grouped into broader categories inspired by the Human Phenotype Ontology (HPO) to facilitate higher-level interpretation.
